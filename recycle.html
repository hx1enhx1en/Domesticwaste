What does “recycle” mean?

Recycle means to process used materials, such as glass, paper, or plastic, into new products to prevent waste and reduce the consumption of fresh raw materials.

  • Rinse out food containers and bottles to prevent contamination
  • Separate paper, plastic, glass and metal into distinct categories
  • Flatten cardboard boxes completely to maximize space in your bin
  • Take hazardous electronics and batteries to specialized local drop-off centers
