Hello we are from EDUSN Thingangyun Campus Year 7 Group-3

  • We created this website to raise awareness about domestic waste and how it affects our environment and daily lives.
  • Domestic waste includes plastic bottles, food waste, paper, and other rubbish that people throw away every day. If waste is not managed properly, it can cause pollution, harm animals, and damage the environment.
  • Through this website, we hope people can learn the causes, consequences, and solutions of domestic waste and help keep our community clean and sustainable.
